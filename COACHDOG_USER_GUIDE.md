# For Coaches

## Coach Setup & Onboarding

### Getting Started
**URL:** `https://coachdog.com/coach-signup` (or your production URL)

### Step 1: Create Your Account

1. **Navigate to Coach Sign Up**
   - Go to the CoachDog homepage
   - Click "For Coaches" in the navigation menu
   - Click "Sign Up as a Coach" or "Get Started"

2. **Enter Your Details**
   - **Name:** Your full professional name (as you want clients to see it)
   - **Email:** Professional email address (used for login and client inquiries)
   - **Password:** Secure password (minimum 8 characters)
   - **Accept Terms:** Review and accept Terms of Service

3. **Email Verification**
   - Check your email inbox for verification link
   - Click the verification link to activate your account
   - You'll be redirected to complete your profile

### Step 2: Complete Your Coach Profile

Once logged in, you'll be taken to your **Coach Dashboard** where you can complete your profile.

#### Profile Photo
- **Requirement:** Professional headshot
- **Recommended:**
  - Square aspect ratio (1:1)
  - Minimum 400x400 pixels
  - Clear, well-lit photo
  - Professional attire
  - Neutral or clean background
- **File Formats:** JPG, PNG
- **Max Size:** 5MB

#### Basic Information

**Location & Availability**
1. **Base Location:**
   - Select from dropdown of 50+ UK cities
   - Choose "Remote" if you work online-only
   - Select "Other" to enter a custom location

2. **Travel Radius** (for in-person coaching):
   - Within 5 miles
   - Within 10 miles
   - Within 25 miles
   - Within 50 miles
   - Nationwide (UK)
   - International

3. **Coaching Format:**
   - ☐ In-Person Coaching
   - ☐ Online Coaching (via Zoom, Teams, etc.)
   - ☐ Hybrid (both in-person and online)

**Tip:** Offering online coaching increases your reach to clients nationwide and globally!

**Pricing**
- **Hourly Rate:** Your standard rate per session
- **Currency:** Select your preferred currency (defaults to GBP)
  - Available: GBP, USD, EUR, JPY, AUD, CAD, CHF, CNY, INR, NZD
- **Note:** Rates are displayed to clients in your chosen currency

**Experience**
- Years of coaching experience (0-50+ years)
- Displayed as experience level to clients

#### Professional Details

**Specialties** (Select all that apply)
- Career Growth
- Stress Relief
- Relationships
- Health & Wellness
- Executive Coaching
- General Coaching

**Coaching Expertise** (Optional - Advanced)
Choose from 7 categories:
1. **Career & Professional Development**
   - Career Transition, Leadership Development, Executive Coaching, Team Coaching, etc.
2. **Business & Entrepreneurship**
   - Business Start-up, Business Growth & Scaling, Strategic Planning, Sales Coaching, etc.
3. **Health & Wellness**
   - Stress Management, Mindfulness, Nutrition, Fitness, Mental Health, etc.
4. **Personal & Life**
   - Life Purpose, Goal Setting, Relationship Coaching, Parenting, etc.
5. **Financial**
   - Financial Planning, Debt Management, Investment Strategy, etc.
6. **Niche & Demographic**
   - Women in Leadership, LGBTQ+ Coaching, Youth Coaching, etc.
7. **Methodology & Modality**
   - NLP, CBT-Based Coaching, Positive Psychology, etc.

**Languages Offered**
- Select all languages in which you offer coaching
- Common: English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, etc.

**CPD Qualifications** (Optional)
- Add your professional development certifications
- Examples: ACC, PCC, MCC, Diploma in Coaching, etc.

**Gender** (Optional)
- Some clients prefer coaches of a specific gender
- Options: Male, Female, Non-binary, Prefer not to say

#### Biography

**Your Bio** (Essential for attracting clients)
- **Length:** 150-500 words recommended
- **Include:**
  - Your coaching philosophy
  - What makes you unique
  - Types of clients you work best with
  - Your coaching approach/methodology
  - Key achievements or transformations you've facilitated
  - Personal touch (appropriate background that builds connection)

**Tips for a Great Bio:**
- Write in first person ("I help clients...")
- Be authentic and personable
- Focus on client benefits, not just credentials
- Include specific examples or success stories
- Avoid jargon - write for your ideal client
- Proofread carefully

#### Accreditation

**Accreditation Body**
- None (if not accredited)
- EMCC (European Mentoring & Coaching Council)
- ICF (International Coaching Federation)
- Other

**If EMCC or ICF:**
- **Accreditation Level:**
  - Foundation
  - Practitioner
  - Senior Practitioner
  - Master Practitioner
- **Profile URL:** Link to your public EMCC or ICF profile
  - Example: `https://www.emccouncil.org/eu/en/find/profile/12345`
  - Example: `https://coachfederation.org/find-a-coach/profile/12345`

**Verification Process:**
- Paste your public accreditation profile URL
- Our system will verify your accreditation within 24-48 hours
- Once verified, you'll receive a verified badge on your profile
- Clients can click your badge to view your official accreditation

#### Social Links & Contact

**Add Your Professional Links:**
- LinkedIn profile
- Instagram (professional account)
- Facebook business page
- YouTube channel
- Website
- Twitter/X
- TikTok (if coaching-related)

**Contact Methods:**
- Email (auto-populated from account email)
- Phone number (optional, for WhatsApp integration)
- Booking link (Calendly, Cal.com, etc.)

**How Contact Works:**
- Email and phone are managed through a "Contact" button
- Clients click "Contact" to reveal your contact options
- WhatsApp button appears automatically if you add a phone number
- Social links appear as icon buttons on your profile

### Step 3: Review & Publish

1. **Preview Your Profile**
   - Use "Preview Profile" button to see how clients will view you
   - Check all information is accurate and professional
   - Ensure photo displays correctly

2. **Profile Status**
   - **Draft:** Profile not visible to clients (while you're setting up)
   - **Published:** Profile live and searchable by clients
   - Toggle status in your dashboard

3. **Save Changes**
   - Always click "Save Changes" after editing
   - You'll see a confirmation message when saved successfully

2. Click dropdown to change city

- LinkedIn, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, Twitter, TikTok, Website, Calendly, Cal.com, Custom

### Managing Your Reviews

#### Viewing Reviews

1. Navigate to your dashboard
2. Scroll to "Reviews" section
3. See all client reviews with:
   - Star rating (1-5 stars)
   - Review text
   - Client name (or anonymous)
   - Date posted

#### Responding to Reviews

1. Find the review you want to respond to
2. Click "Reply" button
3. Write a thoughtful, professional response
4. Click "Post Reply"

**Best Practices for Replies:**
- Thank clients for positive reviews
- Address concerns in negative reviews professionally
- Keep responses brief and authentic
- Avoid being defensive
- Demonstrate professionalism and care

**Example Responses:**

**Positive Review:**
> "Thank you, Sarah! It's been a pleasure working with you on your career transition. I'm thrilled to hear about your new role and excited to continue supporting your growth. Best wishes!"

**Negative Review:**
> "Thank you for your feedback, Mark. I'm sorry to hear the sessions didn't meet your expectations. I'd welcome the opportunity to discuss this further and understand how I can improve. Please feel free to reach out directly."

#### Review Guidelines

**Reviews You CAN:**
- Reply to any review
- Thank clients for feedback
- Professionally address concerns

**Reviews You CANNOT:**
- Delete reviews (only clients or platform admins can)
- Edit reviews
- Report spam/abusive reviews (contact support)

**Getting More Reviews:**
- After successful coaching engagements, politely ask clients to leave a review
- Include review request in your post-coaching follow-up
- Make it easy: "If you found our sessions valuable, I'd appreciate a review on CoachDog!"

## Accreditation Verification

### Why Get Verified?

**Benefits:**
1. **Trust Badge** - Official verification badge on your profile
2. **Higher Rankings** - Verified coaches appear higher in search results
3. **Client Confidence** - Clients prefer verified, accredited coaches
4. **Clickable Link** - Clients can verify your credentials directly on EMCC/ICF website

### Supported Accreditation Bodies

#### EMCC (European Mentoring & Coaching Council)
- Foundation
- Practitioner
- Senior Practitioner
- Master Practitioner

#### ICF (International Coaching Federation)
- ACC (Associate Certified Coach)
- PCC (Professional Certified Coach)
- MCC (Master Certified Coach)

#### Other Accreditation
- Currently displayed but not auto-verified
- Manual verification may be available (contact support)

### How to Get Verified

#### Step 1: Ensure You Have a Public Profile

**EMCC:**
1. Log into your EMCC account
2. Ensure your profile is set to "Public"
3. Copy your public profile URL
   - Example: `https://www.emccouncil.org/eu/en/find/profile/12345`

**ICF:**
1. Log into your ICF account
2. Ensure you're listed in the public "Find a Coach" directory
3. Copy your public profile URL
   - Example: `https://coachfederation.org/find-a-coach/profile/12345`

#### Step 2: Add to CoachDog Profile

1. Log into your Coach Dashboard
2. Scroll to "Accreditation" section
3. Select your accreditation body (EMCC or ICF)
4. Choose your accreditation level
5. Paste your public profile URL
6. Click "Save Changes"

#### Step 3: Verification Process

1. Our system automatically checks your profile URL
2. Verification typically completes within 24-48 hours
3. You'll receive an email when verified
4. Verified badge appears on your profile automatically

#### Step 4: Displaying Your Verification

Once verified, your profile will show:
- **EMCC Badge** (navy blue with gold accents)
- **ICF Badge** (blue with official ICF colors)
- Badge includes:
  - Accreditation body logo
  - Verification checkmark
  - Your accreditation level (e.g., "PCC", "Senior Practitioner")
  - Clickable link: "Check out my ICF/EMCC accreditation here"

### Troubleshooting Verification

**Verification Failed - Common Issues:**

1. **Profile URL Not Public**
   - Solution: Log into EMCC/ICF and set profile to public/visible

2. **URL Incorrect Format**
   - Solution: Copy the exact URL from your browser when viewing your profile
   - Must be your specific profile URL, not general EMCC/ICF homepage

3. **Name Mismatch**
   - Solution: Ensure your CoachDog name matches your EMCC/ICF profile name
   - Contact support if you use a different professional name

4. **Accreditation Expired**
   - Solution: Renew your accreditation with EMCC/ICF first
   - Then re-submit for verification

**Still Having Issues?**
- Contact support: support@coachdog.com
- Include: Your profile URL, accreditation body, and level
- We'll manually verify within 2 business days

## Managing Reviews & Reputation

### Understanding the Review System

**Star Ratings:**
- 1 Star: Poor
- 2 Stars: Fair
- 3 Stars: Good
- 4 Stars: Very Good
- 5 Stars: Excellent

**Your Profile Shows:**
- Average rating (e.g., 4.8 stars)
- Total number of reviews
- Clickable to scroll to reviews section

### How Clients Leave Reviews

1. Client must have your profile URL or find you in search
2. Click "Leave a Review" on your profile
3. Select star rating (1-5 stars)
4. Write review text (optional but encouraged)
5. Choose to post as:
   - Named (shows their name)
   - Anonymous (shows "Anonymous Client")

**Note:** Clients can edit or delete their reviews within 30 days of posting.

### Responding to Reviews

See "Managing Your Reviews" section above for detailed guidelines.

**Encourage Reviews:**
- Ask satisfied clients after successful engagements
- Make it part of your closing process
- Provide the direct link to your profile

**Respond to All Reviews:**
- Thank positive reviews
- Address concerns in constructive reviews
- Shows you value feedback
- Demonstrates professionalism

**Maintain Quality:**
- Consistent positive reviews build trust
- Aim for 4.5+ average rating
- More reviews = more credibility

**Handle Negative Reviews:**
- Respond professionally and empathetically
- Acknowledge concerns
- Offer to discuss offline
- Never argue or be defensive
- Use as learning opportunity

## Coach FAQs

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes, contact support@coachdog.com with your current and new email address.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support@coachdog.com. Note: Deleting your account removes your profile and all reviews.

### Profile & Listing

**Q: How long does it take for my profile to go live?**
A: Immediately after you click "Publish" in your dashboard. Draft profiles are not visible to clients.

**Q: Can I have multiple profiles?**
A: No, each coach is limited to one profile per account.

**Q: Why isn't my profile showing in search results?**
A: Check:
1. Profile is set to "Published" (not Draft)
2. You've completed all required fields
3. Your specialties/filters match client searches
4. Your location is set correctly

**Q: Can I edit my profile after publishing?**
A: Yes, you can edit anytime. Changes are saved immediately.

**Q: What's the difference between Specialty and Coaching Expertise?**
A:
- **Specialty:** Broad categories (Career, Wellness, etc.) - Required
- **Coaching Expertise:** Detailed, specific areas (e.g., "Career Transition", "Stress Management") - Optional but recommended for better matching

### Pricing & Payments

**Q: Does CoachDog take a commission on my bookings?**
A: No, CoachDog is a directory/matching platform. All payments are handled directly between you and your clients.

**Q: Can I offer different rates for different services?**
A: Currently, profiles show one hourly rate. You can mention variable pricing in your bio.

**Q: What currency should I use?**
A: Use your local currency. International clients will see a currency conversion notice.

### Accreditation & Verification

**Q: Do I need to be accredited to join CoachDog?**
A: No! Accreditation is optional but recommended as it builds trust and improves your ranking.

**Q: My accreditation verification is pending. How long will it take?**
A: 24-48 hours typically. If it's been longer, check your email for notifications or contact support.

**Q: I'm accredited with another body (not EMCC/ICF). Can I get verified?**
A: Select "Other" and contact support for manual verification options.

**Q: Can I update my accreditation level later?**
A: Yes, update your profile anytime. Re-verification may be required.

### Reviews & Ratings

**Q: Can I delete a negative review?**
A: No, only clients or platform admins can remove reviews. You can reply professionally and contact support if a review violates guidelines.

**Q: How do I report a fake or abusive review?**
A: Contact support@coachdog.com with the review details.

**Q: Can clients leave reviews anonymously?**
A: Yes, clients can choose to post anonymously.

**Q: Do old reviews expire?**
A: No, reviews remain on your profile indefinitely unless removed by the client or admin.

### Technical Issues

**Q: My photo won't upload. What's wrong?**
A: Check:
- File size under 5MB
- Format is JPG or PNG
- Try a different browser
- Contact support if issue persists

**Q: Changes aren't saving. What should I do?**
A:
1. Click "Save Changes" button
2. Check your internet connection
3. Try refreshing the page
4. Clear browser cache
5. Contact support if issue continues

**Q: The platform looks broken/weird on my phone.**
A: Try:
- Update your mobile browser
- Clear browser cache
- Use Chrome or Safari
- Report specific issues to support

## Using the Matching Quiz

### What is the Matching Quiz?

The CoachDog Matching Quiz is an intelligent questionnaire designed to help you find coaches who match your specific needs, goals, and preferences.

**Benefits:**
- Personalized coach recommendations
- Save time searching
- See match percentages for each coach
- Get coaches ranked by compatibility

### Accessing the Quiz

**URL:** `https://coachdog.com/quiz` or `https://coachdog.com/get-matched`

**From Homepage:**
1. Click "Get Matched" button (prominent on homepage)
2. Click "Find Your Perfect Coach" in hero section
3. Navigate to "Find a Coach" → "Take the Quiz"

### Taking the Quiz

#### Step 1: What Brings You Here?

**Select Your Primary Coaching Need:**
- Career Growth
- Stress Relief
- Relationships
- Health & Wellness
- Executive/Leadership Coaching
- General Life Coaching

**Why This Matters:**
Coaches specialize in different areas. Your selection helps us match you with coaches who focus on your primary need.

**How Would You Like to Work?**
- ☐ In-Person (face-to-face sessions)
- ☐ Online (video calls via Zoom, Teams, etc.)

**What's Your Budget Per Session?**

- Drag to set your maximum rate
- Default: £100/hour

**Tip:** Coaching rates vary widely. Quality coaches exist at all price points. Consider your budget realistically - coaching is an investment in yourself.

**Where Are You Located?**

- Select "Remote" if online-only

**How Far Can You Travel?**
- Within 5 miles
- Within 10 miles
- Within 25 miles
- Within 50 miles
- Nationwide

**Only shown if:** You selected "In-Person" or "Hybrid" coaching format.

**Minimum Coaching Experience Required:**

**Consider:**
- New coaches (0-5 years): Often more affordable, fresh perspectives, eager to help
- Experienced coaches (10+ years): Proven track record, deep expertise, higher rates
- Highly experienced (20+ years): Master coaches, premium rates, specialized expertise

#### Step 6: Advanced Preferences (Optional)

**Language Preference:**
- English, Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, etc.
- Select if you need coaching in a specific language
- Default: No preference (English assumed)

**Coaching Expertise (Detailed Areas):**
Choose from 7 categories:
- Career & Professional Development
- Business & Entrepreneurship
- Health & Wellness
- Personal & Life
- Financial

- Methodology & Modality

- Career: "Career Transition", "Leadership Development", "Interview Preparation"

- Life: "Life Purpose", "Goal Setting", "Relationship Coaching"

**Professional Qualifications:**
- CPD certifications (ACC, PCC, MCC, etc.)

- Default: No preference

**Gender Preference:**
- Male
- Female

### Viewing Your Results

#### Match Score Explained

After completing the quiz, you'll see coaches ranked by **Match Score:**

- **100% Match** - Perfect match (meets all your criteria)
- **75-99% Match** - Close match (meets most criteria)
- **51-74% Match** - Partial match (meets some criteria)
- **Below 50%** - Not shown (too few matches)

**Match Score Considers:**
- Specialty match
- Format availability (in-person, online, hybrid)
- Price within your budget
- Experience meets your minimum
- Location + travel radius (if in-person)
- Language offered
- Coaching expertise areas
- Professional qualifications
- Gender preference

#### Results Page

**You'll See:**
- Total number of matching coaches
- Coaches grouped by match percentage:

**Q: Can I see coaches outside the UK?**
A: Currently, CoachDog focuses on UK-based coaches, but many offer online sessions internationally.

**Q: How many coaches should I contact?**
A: We recommend contacting 2-4 coaches. Most offer free discovery calls so you can assess fit before committing.

**Q: Do I pay through CoachDog?**
A: No, CoachDog is a matching/directory platform. All payments are handled directly between you and your coach.

**Q: Why are prices in different currencies?**

**Q: Are there any fees for using CoachDog?**

A: Some coaches offer sliding scales or package discounts. Ask during your discovery call.

**Q: How do I contact a coach?**

**Q: How do I know if a coach is a good fit?**

  5. Refresh page

**Problem: Coach profile won't load**
- Solution:
  1. Refresh page
  2. Clear browser cache
  3. Try different browser
  4. Check internet connection
  5. Contact support if persists

**Problem: Can't see reviews**
- Solution:
  1. Scroll down to "Reviews" tab
  2. Click "Reviews" tab if on mobile
  3. Coach may have no reviews yet (new profile)

#### Quiz Issues

**Problem: Quiz won't submit**
- Solution:
  1. Ensure all required questions answered
  2. Check internet connection
  3. Try different browser
  4. Clear cache and retry

**Problem: Match results seem wrong**
- Solution:
  1. Review your quiz answers
  2. Retake quiz if needed
  3. Use filters to refine results
  4. Browse all coaches without match scoring

#### Contact Issues

**Problem: Email bounce back**
- Solution:
  1. Verify email address is correct
  2. Check spam folder for responses
  3. Try contacting via phone/WhatsApp
  4. Use booking link if available

**Problem: Coach not responding**
- Solution:
  1. Wait 2-3 business days
  2. Check spam folder
  3. Try alternative contact method
  4. Contact another coach
  5. Some coaches may not be actively checking

#### Technical Issues

**Problem: Site looks broken on mobile**
- Solution:
  1. Update mobile browser
  2. Clear browser cache
  3. Try Chrome or Safari
  4. Disable browser extensions
  5. Report to support

**Problem: Images not loading**
- Solution:
  1. Check internet connection
  2. Refresh page
  3. Clear browser cache
  4. Try different network (WiFi vs mobile data)

**Problem: Filters not working**
- Solution:
  1. Click "Apply Filters" button
  2. Refresh page
  3. Try different browser
  4. Clear cache
  5. Contact support

### Clearing Cache

**Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Click "Clear data"

**Safari:**
1. Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
2. Click "Remove All"

**Mobile:**
- iOS: Settings → Safari → Clear History and Website Data
- Android: Settings → Apps → Chrome → Storage → Clear Cache
